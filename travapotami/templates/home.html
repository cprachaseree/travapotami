  • question_answerWonder what TRAVAPOTAMI means?
    TRAVEL + HIPPOPOTAMI = TRAVAPOTAMI
    Travapotami comes from a combination of 2 words: Travel and Hippopotami. (Why Hippopotami? Press on the next question for the answer.) "Travapotami" is a web application designed for users who wish to find travel companions that share similar interests and personality traits. Whether it is a trip to a local natural heritage site or a trip to the other side of the world “Travapotami” covers all kinds of trips. Anyone can either create or join a trip, the hosts of the trips are required to write a detailed description of the entire trip including information such as destination, budget, length and type of trip.
  • nature_peopleWhat inspires chosen animal?

    The lucky animal we chose as our web ambassador is Hippopotamus, where the biggest reason being hippopotami live in groups of 15 or more animals. As a travelling web app, we believe meeting and travelling with group of campanions add memories and laughter to your journey.

    DID YOU KNOW?
